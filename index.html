ABOUT COOKED IRL
No filter. No sugarcoating. Just you, ten questions, and the truth about where you're at in life right now.
Cooked IRL is a Gen-Z life audit that checks you across the categories that actually matter — money, mental health, body, and love — and gives you a real verdict.
It's not a test. There's no failing. It's just a look in the mirror with a funny title at the end and some actual tips to help you move.
New categories drop regularly. Stay cooked. Stay aware.
